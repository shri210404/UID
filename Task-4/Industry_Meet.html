Cyber Industry Meet





This event provides a platform for eminent people from the corporate world to share their knowledge and experience with other industries, academia as well as students. The agenda of conducting the event is to have a fruitful discussion on the challenges the industry is facing in the security domain and the role of academia in providing solutions and support to them. The event consists of representations from Industries like NEC, Zoho, and Tata Consultancy Services and Ashok Leyland.

The program will include Inauguration and invited Talk from international speaker followed by company/industry presentations and open discussions.
This event will open opportunities for budding scientists to start new research areas with industry collaboration.
